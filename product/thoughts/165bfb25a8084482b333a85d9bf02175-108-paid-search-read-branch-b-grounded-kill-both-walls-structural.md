## Two independent structural walls (vendor-independent — kill EVERY query-intent search network)
1. **Offer-category wall.** GBP-suspension reinstatement help = "third-party account recovery for a consumer Google product." Google Ads Other-restricted-businesses -> third-party consumer technical support policy (support.google.com/adspolicy/answer/13527027 ; /9479396) EXPLICITLY prohibits "account recovery, password resets or login support" by third parties — PROHIBITED, not certifiable. "Google Business Profile" in ad copy also trips trademark policy. Microsoft Advertising mirrors via restricted-business + IP policy. No major query-intent search network will serve this offer regardless of budget/identity.
2. **Identity-provisioning wall** (= recorded Google-account-provisioning failure pattern, now confirmed structural for ad accounts). Google Ads mandatory advertiser identity verification on ALL new accounts (2026 US rollout): 3–7 business days typical, up to 2+ weeks, requires matching legal-entity documents the workspace does not have. Bing new-account verification multi-day–multi-week + billing identity. "Days not weeks" = FALSE.

Precondition 1 (CPC/volume): hard numbers live only behind Keyword Planner/Ahrefs/Semrush; Keyword Planner itself gated behind the same Google Ads account wall. Qualitative: niche distress query + agency-saturated SERP => elevated CPC, low volume; $40 ~= 3–8 clicks, $120 ceiling ~= 8–15 = recorded n=5 underpowered-cohort anti-pattern. Underpowered even if walls 1–2 did not exist.
